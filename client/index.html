Fairmont Hotels

Exclusive 5-star hotels for dream vacation

Fairmont Hotel is situated on an attractive location, next to the sea, river, main beach and the old town. Fairmont Hotel offers its guests a warm and friendly atmosphere, high quality facilities and services that will satisfy your need for relaxation and rest from everyday life in a comfortable Mediterranean environment. Our friendly and professional staff are here to satisfy all your needs. We look forward to your visit.

Fairmont Hotel is a new hotel that will receive visitors for the first time in April 2017. It is located at a distance of 3 km to the lovely pirate town Omiš. The hotel offers its guests 103 modern and spacious rooms with sea view and balcony, as well as indoor and outdoor swimming pool, pool bar, restaurant with show cooking and much more. The best choice for your well-deserved vacation.

Modern Rooms

Best Dreams Happen when you are awake

Fairmont accommodation in comfortable and elegantly decorated rooms, exceptionally high-quality gastronomic offer in the buffet restaurant with a beautiful terrace overlooking the sea, and a traditional pirate tavern with a rich offer from the Mediterranean cuisine. Spend relaxing moments in our wellness center, fitness room, or outside on the roof terrace with a jacuzzi, which offers a breathtaking view of the surrounding islands, sea, mountains and town fortifications.

Rich Gastronomy Offer

There is no sincere love than the love of food

The perfect interplay of light, wood, portholes, a feeling of a time long gone, pirates on deck ready for an extraordinary gastronomic pleasure, under the modern direction of the chef. Buffet and Andante Restaurant Plaža is located on the ground floor and is the ideal place to spend pleasant evenings, to make new acquaintances and enjoy a wide selection of local and international dishes.
